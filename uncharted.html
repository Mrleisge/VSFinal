Uncharted was developed by Naughty Dog and was released in 2007 as an action-adventure third-person shooter. It focuses on a treasure hunter named Nathan Drake, a news reporter and Nate's love interest Elena Fisher, and Victor "Sully" Sullivan, Nate's mentor and father figure. It draws heavily from the style of classic action-adventure movies like Indiana Jones, featuring high-paced action, ancient mysteries, and the occasional supernatural twist.

There have been 5 main games:
Uncharted: Drake's Fortune (2007)
Uncharted 2: Among Thieves (2009)
Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception (2011)
Uncharted 4: A Thief's End (2016)
Uncharted: The Lost Legacy (2017)
There have also been 2 spinoffs made for the PS Vita and even a movie released in 2022.

Uncharted captured the eyes of so many because of the thriling action, The new gen freerunning and captivating story.

Uncharted has a wide variety of recognisable characters:
Nathan Drake - The main protagonist and treasure hunter.
Elena Fisher - A journalist and Nathan's love interest.
Victor "Sully" Sullivan - Nathan's mentor and father figure.
Chloe Frazer - A fellow treasure hunter and Nathan's ally.
Nadine Ross - An antagonist in Uncharted 4 and The Lost Legacy.
Sam Drake - Nathan's long-lost brother, introduced in Uncharted 4.
