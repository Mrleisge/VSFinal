The Last of Us is a post-apocalyptic action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and released in 2013. It follows the journey of Joel, a smuggler tasked with bringing a teenage girl across the country faceing several dangers, including fungus infected humans and hostile survivors.

What people love about The Last of Us is the emotional depth, character development, and the moral dilemmas that players face throughout the game. The story is rich and immersive, making it a standout title in the gaming world.

The main characters are:
Joel - The main protagonist, a smuggler with a troubled past.
Ellie - The second portagonist who is a teenage girl with a mysterious immunity to the infection.
Tess - Joel's partner and a fellow smuggler.
Tommy - Joel's brother and a former member of the Fireflies.
Marlene - The leader of the Fireflies, a revolutionary militia group.
Bill - A paranoid survivalist who helps Joel and Ellie on their journey.
Abby - A new character introduced in the sequel, with her own motivations and backstory.
